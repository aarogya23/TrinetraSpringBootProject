F1 24 is a 2024 Formula One racing simulation video game developed and published by EA Sports and Codemasters. It is the latest installment in the official Formula 1 series, following F1 23, and delivers the thrill of the world’s most prestigious motorsport like never before. Featuring authentic circuits, updated driver line-ups, and advanced racing physics, F1 24 pushes the limits of realism with stunning visuals, immersive handling, and the true-to-life atmosphere of Formula 1.

Career Mode

F1 24 introduces a deeper Career Mode with expanded driver reputation systems, contract negotiations, and team dynamics. Whether you rise as a rookie chasing your first podium or lead a championship team to glory, every decision on and off the track shapes your Formula 1 journey.

My Team

Create, customize, and manage your very own F1 team in the enhanced My Team mode. From designing liveries and managing budgets to signing drivers and developing car performance, the choices you make will determine your path to becoming a racing powerhouse.

Multiplayer & Esports

Race online against players worldwide in thrilling head-to-head competitions. With improved matchmaking, cross-play support, and official esports events, F1 24 delivers the ultimate competitive racing experience for both casual players and professional drivers alike.
