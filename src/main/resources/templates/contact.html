Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get technical support?

You can get technical support through multiple channels: use our 24/7 live chat, submit a support ticket through the contact form, email us at support@trinetra.games, or call our support hotline during business hours.

What are your support hours?

Our live chat and email support are available 24/7. Phone support is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Nepal Time. We typically respond to emails within 24 hours.

How can I report a bug or technical issue?

To report bugs, please use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Include detailed information about the issue, your system specifications, and steps to reproduce the problem for faster resolution.

Can I suggest new features or games?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. Use the contact form and select "Feature Request" to share your ideas. We review all suggestions and consider them for future updates.

How do I contact media or press relations?

For media inquiries, interviews, or press releases, please select "Press & Media" in the contact form or email us directly at press@trinetra.games. We'll connect you with our media relations team.

Are you looking for business partnerships?

Yes! We're always interested in exploring partnerships with developers, publishers, and other gaming companies. Select "Partnership Inquiry" in the contact form or email partnerships@trinetra.games.
