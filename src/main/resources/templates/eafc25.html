Game Description

EA Sports FC 25 is a 2025 football simulation video game developed and published by EA Sports. It is the latest installment in the EA Sports FC series, following EA Sports FC 24, and continues the legacy of the long-running FIFA franchise. Featuring enhanced gameplay mechanics, updated team rosters, and deeper career and Ultimate Team modes, EA FC 25 delivers the most authentic and immersive football experience to date. With improved graphics, dynamic animations, and real-world football innovations, it sets a new standard for the beautiful game in virtual form.

Career Mode

EA Sports FC 25 expands the Career Mode experience with more immersive management features, dynamic storylines, and deeper player development. Whether you’re managing a top-tier club or rising through the ranks as a young star, your choices shape your path to greatness on and off the pitch.

Ultimate Team

Build your dream squad in Ultimate Team with new Evolutions, refined chemistry, and fresh ICONs and Heroes. FC 25 introduces more customization options and seasonal challenges, allowing players to create a team that reflects their playstyle and vision.

Clubs & Volta

Clubs mode returns with cross-play support, expanded progression, and new customization features to represent your club your way. In Volta, street football reaches new heights with vibrant environments, creative flair, and fast-paced gameplay that captures the spirit of the global game.
